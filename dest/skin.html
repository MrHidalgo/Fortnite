The Red Knight is an outfit in the Fortnite: Battle Royale and is a part of the Fort Knights Set. The outfit was available for 1200 V-Bucks before the Season 2 of the game began. The Red Knight Outfit also appears rarely in the Daily Item Shop for 2000 V-Bucks.

Design

Cover with red details and outlines, this black suit resembles the theme of the second season of the Battle Pass, but it is actually not a part of it. The Red Knight outfit is the Red Menace of Anarchy Acres of the Fort Knights Set.
